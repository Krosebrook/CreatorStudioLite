#### Enforcement Guidelines

**Level 1: Correction**
- Community Impact: Minor inappropriate language
- Consequence: Private warning, public apology request

**Level 2: Warning**
- Community Impact: Violation through single incident
- Consequence: Warning with consequences for continued behavior

**Level 3: Temporary Ban**
- Community Impact: Serious violation or sustained inappropriate behavior
- Consequence: Temporary ban from community interactions

**Level 4: Permanent Ban**
- Community Impact: Pattern of violations, harassment, aggression
- Consequence: Permanent ban from community
